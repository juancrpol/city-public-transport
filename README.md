# Public Transport with AI

Final project for the Building AI course

## Summary

This project is designed to improve the mobility of the city of Neiva. By knowing public transport routes, city sectors and mobile devices, users can access the appropriate routes to quickly reach their destination.


## Background

* This project solves the mobilization problems of the city of Neiva.
* During the course of the day, adults or foreigners do not know the appropriate routes to reach their destination.
* My personal motivation is to contribute to the city's public problems, improving mobility problems and setting an example in the face of the precarious situation of the city's public sector.
* This is important since it allows the flow of public transport to be optimal and adequate for a city like Neiva, which is the capital of the department of Huila.


## How is it used?

It is used by using an application for mobile devices. During the day, users will be able to access the most appropriate routes to reach their destination using artificial intelligence. The users are all people residing in the city of Neiva, especially people of legal age and foreigners who do not know the city.

## Challenges

The project does not solve mobility problems in restricted areas such as townships where the necessary information is scarce.

Regarding ethical issues, we must take into account the biases that artificial intelligence may generate when recommending routes avoiding higher risk or lower-income areas, something that can happen

## What next?

The project can grow by replicating locally in restricted areas such as townships and expanding nationally to cities that have not implemented a viable public transportation network.
